Schandry Task

Instructions: Breathe normally and try and sense your heart beating without taking your pulse. You can close your eyes if you prefer. For each trial, when you are prompted to "Start" begin counting each heartbeat you feel. When you are prompted to "Stop", input the number of beats you counted. Do not guess how many heart beats you think are occurring, rather respond to how many you can actually feel. If you cannot feel your heartbeat enter '0'. In between trials you will be asked to state how confident you are in your answer.
